Repatriation FAQ'S

Repatriation services involve the process of returning the body of a deceased person to their home country after they have passed away abroad.

Repatriation is often necessary when individuals die while traveling, living, or working in a foreign country, and their families wish to have them buried or cremated in their home country.

The time frame for repatriation can vary depending on the country, the cause of death, and the completion of required documentation. It generally takes between a few days to a couple of weeks.

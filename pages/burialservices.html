Burial Services in Kenya

We provide professional and compassionate burial services in Kenya with reputable and licensed funeral directors.

Burying a loved one is always an emotional experience. The nature of the service itself can be modified in accordance with the deceased’s wishes, or with the general preferences of the family.

We do not take a one-size-fits-all approach to our burial services, but rather we create the kind of mood that families request—while ensuring that, regardless of the particulars, each burial service is intimate, affirming, and beautiful.

01

What we provide

  • We provide immediate burials without public ceremonies, visitations, viewings, or wakes with either closed or open caskets, and funeral services at locations such as our funeral home, churches, private residences, or cemetery gravesides.
  • You have the choice of selecting a public service, a private service, or a combination, based on your wishes and how you prefer to honor your loved one.
02

Planning a burial service

  • Required documents
  • Selection of cemetery and burial property
  • Choosing a coffin/casket and hearse
  • Planning the details of the graveside service
